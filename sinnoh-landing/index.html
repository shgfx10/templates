FAQ

Trainer questions

Which Sinnoh starter should I pick?

All three are viable! Infernape is great for offense, Empoleon for defense, and Torterra for balanced teams. Choose based on your playstyle.

Where do I find the Move Tutor?

Move Tutors are scattered across Sinnoh - check the underground, Stark Mountain, and the Route 212 house. Some require Battle Points.

How do I get Dialga/Palkia?

After defeating the Elite Four, visit the Spear Pillar. Your version determines which legendary you'll encounter.

Is online trading safe?

Yes! Our Trade Hub uses secure trading with verification systems to prevent scams and ensure fair trades.
